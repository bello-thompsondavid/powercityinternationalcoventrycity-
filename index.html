About Our Church

PowerCity International - Coventry Campus

Welcome to PowerCity International, where we are committed to reintroducing Jesus to this generation. Under the leadership of Dr. Abel Damina, we strive to create an atmosphere where believers can grow in their knowledge of Christ and their identity in Him.

Our Coventry Campus is a vibrant community of believers dedicated to worship, fellowship, and the study of God's Word. We believe in equipping believers to understand who they are in Christ, what they have in Christ, and what they can do through Christ.
